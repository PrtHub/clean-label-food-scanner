## What CleanLabel Is

CleanLabel is an AI-powered food scanner for iPhone. Point your camera at any ingredient label — cereal box, salad dressing, baby food, protein bar — and our AI reads it for you. In 3 seconds, you get a clear verdict: **Clean** or **Toxic**, with every harmful ingredient flagged and explained.

It's not another calorie tracker. It's not a barcode scanner. It doesn't care about the front of the package. It reads the fine print on the back — the part most people never read — and tells you what's really in your food.

---

## Why It Exists

The food industry spends billions making products look healthy while filling them with ingredients most people don't recognize. "Natural," "Organic," "Healthy" on the front means nothing when the back of the package lists 30+ chemicals you've never heard of.

A few uncomfortable truths:

- **73% of grocery store products** contain at least one toxic additive
- **177+ chemicals** are legally allowed in US food that are banned in the EU
- The average American consumes **5 pounds of chemical additives per year**
- "Natural Flavors" can legally contain up to **100 undisclosed chemicals**
- "Sugar-Free" products often contain artificial sweeteners that destroy gut bacteria
- Most food dyes (Red 40, Yellow 5) are linked to ADHD and hyperactivity in children

Reading labels takes time. Memorizing ingredient names takes expertise. Most people don't have either. That's the gap CleanLabel fills.

## Core Features

### AI Label Scanner

Point your iPhone camera at any ingredient label. The AI reads every word, checks against 260+ harmful ingredients, and returns a verdict in under 3 seconds. Works on any product, in any language, anywhere in the world. No barcode needed.

### Instant Clean / Toxic Verdict

A big, visual result card tells you immediately if the product is safe or flagged. No guessing. No small print. Green means clean. Red means toxic — with every flagged ingredient listed as a tappable pill.

### Ingredient Education

Every flagged ingredient is tappable. Tap it and you see:

- **What it is** — in plain English, not chemistry textbook language
- **Why it's harmful** — specific health effects, not vague warnings
- **What it does to your body** — gut damage, hormone disruption, cancer risk, allergic reactions
- **Where it's commonly found** — so you know what else to watch for
- **A safer alternative** — what to buy instead

50+ in-depth ingredient guides covering 260+ keywords. If an ingredient isn't in our database, AI researches it in real-time.

### Healthier Alternatives

When a product is flagged as toxic, AI suggests 2-3 real, specific, widely-available cleaner products in the same category. "Instead of this, try that." No vague "shop healthier" — actual brand recommendations you can find on most store shelves.

### Personalized Scanning

During onboarding, you set:

- Your **diet** (Vegan, Keto, Paleo, Gluten-Free, Mediterranean, Whole30, etc.)

- Your **health goals** (Gut health, Weight loss, Energy, Protect my family, etc.)

Every scan is tailored to YOU. An ingredient that's fine for some users gets flagged for others based on these settings. Cross-contamination warnings ("Made in a facility that also processes peanuts") are included automatically.

## What Makes CleanLabel Different

### vs. Yuka

Yuka uses a database of pre-scanned products. If a product isn't in their database, you get nothing. CleanLabel uses AI to read any label — even obscure brands, foreign products, or newly released items. Plus we explain WHY each ingredient is harmful; Yuka just scores it.

### vs. Fooducate

Fooducate gives broad grades (A, B, C, D) without explaining what's wrong. CleanLabel flags specific ingredients, explains the harm, and suggests alternatives. We're surgical; they're generic.

### vs. Think Dirty

Think Dirty is focused on cosmetics. CleanLabel is purpose-built for food, with deep dietary personalization (allergies, restrictions, diet types).

### vs. Reading Labels Yourself

You'd need to memorize 260+ chemical names, learn their alternative marketing terms, cross-reference your diet and allergies, and do it all in 10 seconds while holding a product in a busy aisle. CleanLabel does it in 3.
