# 🚀 CleanLabel: Free Marketing Channels (Zero Spend)

Since CleanLabel is a highly visual and high-utility app, it has great potential for viral organic growth. Here are the top ways to market your app without spending a dollar:

## 1. Short-Form Video (TikTok & Instagram Reels)
**Why?** CleanLabel's "Point-and-Shoot" AI is incredibly satisfying to watch. These platforms reward high-utility visuals.
*   **Strategy**: Take 10-second clips of you scanning everyday grocery items (e.g., "healthy" protein bars or oat milks) and showing the flags for seed oils or hidden sugars.
*   **Hashtags**: `#StopEatingSeedOils`, `#HealthHack`, `#GroceryShopping`, `#Biohacking`, `#CleanLabel`.
*   **Action**: Post 1-2 videos daily. Use trending health-related sounds.

## 2. Product Hunt Launch
**Why?** This is the gold standard for "Show and Tell" for new apps.
*   **Strategy**: Prepare a high-quality "Launch" on Product Hunt. Focus on the **Advanced AI Analysis** (no barcode) and the **Privacy** (Local SQLite) aspect—Hacker/Tech communities love this.
*   **Action**: Create a "Coming Soon" page on PH early to build a notification list.

## 3. Building in Public (X/Twitter)
**Why?** The health-tech and biohacking community on X is very active.
*   **Strategy**: Share updates on the app's growth, new features you're building, and interesting "scanned" products. Tag health influencers like *The Food Babe* or *Dr. James DiNicolantonio* when you find deceptive ingredients.
*   **Action**: Use `#BuildInPublic` and `#IndieHackers`. Engage with health-tech founders.

## 4. Hacker News (Show HN)
**Why?** Highly technical and often health-conscious audience.
*   **Strategy**: Write a detailed, technical post about how you used AI Vision (OpenRouter/Gemini) to replace traditional barcode databases and how you prioritize privacy with local storage.
*   **Action**: Post on Tuesday or Wednesday mornings for maximum visibility. Be prepared to answer deep technical questions!

## 5. Quora & Niche Forums
**Why?** High-intent users looking for answers.
*   **Strategy**: Search for questions like "How can I tell if a product has hidden MSG?" or "Best app for avoiding seed oils." Provide a helpful answer and mention CleanLabel as a tool you built to solve this.
*   **Target Sites**: Quora, MyFitnessPal Forums, Bodybuilding.com forums, and specific Celiac/Gluten-Free boards.

## 6. App Store Optimization (ASO)
**Why?** This is how you get found by people searching in the store.
*   **Strategy**: Ensure your App Store keywords include: `food scanner`, `ingredient analyzer`, `seed oil free`, `toxin detector`, `health app`.
*   **Action**: Use a 100-character keyword field effectively. Make sure your title is descriptive: "CleanLabel: AI Food Scanner."

## 7. Direct Outreach to Health Bloggers/Influencers
**Why?** A single "shoutout" can drive thousands of downloads.
*   **Strategy**: Find micro-influencers (5k-50k followers) in the Keto, Vegan, or Seed Oil Free space. Send them a DM: *"Hey! I love your content on X. I built a free tool for our community to scan labels in seconds. Would love for you to try it—I have a few Pro promo codes if you're interested!"*
*   **Action**: Reach out to 5 influencers daily. Focus on those who actually read labels in their videos.

---

## 📈 Priority Matrix
1.  **High Effort / High Reward**: TikTok/Reels (Visuals are king).
2.  **Low Effort / High Trust**: Hacker News & Product Hunt (Builds authority).
3.  **Passive Growth**: ASO & Niche Forum answers (Long-term organic traffic).
